Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how moneyleaderboard.com ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use our Site. We keep it simple: we collect as little as possible and never sell your data.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a message and make a payment, we store the following: your message text and the dollar amount paid. This information is displayed publicly on the leaderboard. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information unless you contact us directly.

3. Payment Information

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. We never see or store your card number, expiry date, or CVC. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data and can be found at stripe.com/privacy.

4. Public Leaderboard

Messages you submit are displayed publicly on the leaderboard along with the amount paid. Do not include personal information in your message, as it will be visible to all visitors.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on this Site. Stripe may use cookies as part of their payment processing; please refer to their privacy policy for details.

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are stored indefinitely unless removed by us at our discretion. If you would like your message removed, contact us and we will consider your request.

7. Third Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing and Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect data independently under their own privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact us and we will remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at wenglish962@gmail.com.
